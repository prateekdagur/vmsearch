VM Search

Guest Management System

Keep troublemakers and annoying guests away from your hotel with the VM Guest Management System. VM Secure is a real-time search software that helps prevent your property from guests and customers who can damage or harm your property.

DNR

Protect your hotel from troublemakers and guests causing harm to your property with the DNR (Do-Not-Rent) guest lists available on the VM Secure software. With the help of IDproofs, you can check the details about the guest and the reasons why they are on the DNR list before confirming their bookings.

Live Events

If you want other hotel owners not to rent their property to DNR guests, then simply place them on your live events Do-Not-Rent List. The hotel owners will be alerted if a guest tries to book a room again in any hotel within a range of 5KM.
